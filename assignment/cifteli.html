Çifteli

The çifteli is a two-stringed traditional Albanian instrument, producing unique melodic tunes that have echoed through centuries of Albanian culture.

Musical Heritage

The çifteli has been a staple in Northern Albanian folk music, accompanying epic songs and lyrical poetry for generations.

Construction & Tuning

Made from carved wood with two nylon strings, the çifteli is tuned diatonically and played with a small pick or fingers.
