Skanderbeg's Sword

The sword of Gjergj Kastrioti Skanderbeg, Albania’s national hero, symbolizes the enduring spirit and resilience of the Albanian people.

Historical Importance

The sword of Skanderbeg played a crucial role in his battles against the Ottoman Empire. It stands as a symbol of resistance, unity, and national pride in Albanian history.

Design & Craftsmanship

The sword features a beautifully crafted blade and hilt, blending both functionality and elegance, showcasing the remarkable craftsmanship of medieval Albanian artisans.
