Qeleshe

The qeleshe is a traditional Albanian felt cap, proudly worn as a symbol of national identity. Its design varies regionally and represents strength, honor, and heritage.

Historical Significance

The qeleshe dates back centuries, often worn by warriors, villagers, and cultural leaders across Albania and Kosovo. It remains a strong symbol in folk dances and festivals today.

Materials & Crafting

Made entirely of wool, the qeleshe is hand-crafted using traditional felting techniques. The process involves soaking, pressing, and carefully shaping the wool into the iconic dome structure.
